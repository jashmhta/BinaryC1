## ğŸ¤– What is AEO?

Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) is the practice of optimizing content and structured data to be easily understood and cited by AI-powered search engines and conversational AI assistants such as:

- **ChatGPT** (OpenAI)
- **Perplexity AI**
- **Google SGE** (Search Generative Experience)
- **Bing Chat** (Microsoft Copilot)
- **Claude** (Anthropic)
- **Gemini** (Google)

Unlike traditional SEO which focuses on ranking in search results, AEO focuses on being the **direct answer** that AI systems provide to user queries.
