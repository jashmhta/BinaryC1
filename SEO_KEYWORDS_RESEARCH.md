## SEO Best Practices for Financial Services

### 1. E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness)
- Showcase team credentials and experience
- Include case studies and success stories
- Display certifications and regulatory compliance
- Add client testimonials and reviews

### 2. YMYL (Your Money Your Life) Compliance
- Ensure all financial information is accurate
- Include proper disclaimers
- Cite credible sources
- Keep content updated regularly

### 3. Technical SEO
- Fast page load times
- Mobile-responsive design
- Secure HTTPS connection
- Proper URL structure
- XML sitemap
- Schema markup for financial services

### 4. Content Strategy
- Educational blog posts about financial topics
- Industry insights and market analysis
- Service-specific landing pages
- FAQ sections addressing common queries
- Case studies demonstrating expertise

### 5. Local SEO
- Google Business Profile optimization
- Local citations and directories
- Location-specific content
- Mumbai-focused keywords
- Address and contact information consistency
